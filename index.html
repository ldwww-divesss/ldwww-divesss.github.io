Bio

I am currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Intelligent Science and Technology at Fudan University, where I rank 2/27 with a GPA of 3.87/4.0. I am fortunate to work with Prof. Chen Qian, Prof. Zhiyuan Liu, Prof. Xiaozhi Wang, and other collaborators on agentic AI, LLM-based multi-agent systems, and model interpretability.

I am broadly excited about turning LLM agents from engineering artifacts into systems that can be evaluated, diagnosed, improved, and trusted. I am always open to research conversations and collaborations.
